What's this about

In this lesson, you will use the Robonomics service, which queries the telemetry of smart home devices. This allows you to receive data from the Home Assistant remotely.

Instructions

  1. Go to dapp and choose SmartHome Telemetry service. You should see the next:

  2. In the CONTROLLER field enter the SUB_ADMIN address. Insert the seed phrase to encrypt data.

  3. In the Get telemetry block choose a timestamp from the drop-down list and press the DOWNLOAD TELEMETRY button.

    Telemetry downloading could take some time. After finishing, you will see the information from your sensors:
